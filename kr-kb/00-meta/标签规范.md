# Tagging conventions

Three orthogonal dimensions in front matter `tags` array.

## Topic (hierarchical)

- `dl/family`, `dl/constructor`, `dl/reasoning`, `dl/semantics`
- `syntax/manchester`, `syntax/functional`, `syntax/serialization`
- `owl/profile`, `owl/tooling`
- `kg/construction`, `kg/embedding`, `kg/qa`
- `llm/prompting`, `llm/structured-output`, `llm/evaluation`
- `bridge/nl-to-dl`, `bridge/dl-to-nl`, `bridge/neuro-symbolic`

## Type

- `type/concept`, `type/syntax`, `type/tool`, `type/paper`, `type/experiment`, `type/index`

## Status & thesis

- `status/seed`, `status/draft`, `status/evergreen`
- `thesis-relevant` — highlight for thesis writing

## Linking

- `related:` in front matter — explicit graph edges
- `[[wikilink]]` in body — contextual references
- Domain MOC files — curated learning order
